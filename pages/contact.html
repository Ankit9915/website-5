Contact Information

Individual developers are reachable at the following addresses. Note: these are not meant for your questions about a feature, bug, can't get something to work, or want something implemented. You should direct these questions to the forums or in our IRC channel.

Bug reports can be discussed on IRC or forums, but the best place for it to be addressed is on our bug tracker.

Do not address support requests or general questions to individual developers. You will not get an answer this way!

For a more immediate form of communication you can join us in the #openttd channel on OFTC with your favourite IRC client. You can use the channel for questions, problems, development or general discussion.
