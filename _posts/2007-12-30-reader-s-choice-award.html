--- title: Reader's choice award author: Mihamix ---

We are happy to announce our second received award in a hopefully long list to come. OpenTTD has won first prize of the HUP community (HUP = Hungarian Unix Portal) reader's choice contest in category 'Favourite Free Game'. You can read the official announcement here (Hungarian).

This is the second time OpenTTD has been awarded with first prize by the HUP community, last time in 2005.

The accompanying logo will be put up shortly in its own honourable space as soon as I figure out where to put it exactly ;). Until then...
